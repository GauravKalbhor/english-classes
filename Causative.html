Present Indefinet :-

Present Indefinite Tense or Present Tense can be defined as the action that is done in the present however there is no definite time limit given of it being accomplished.
for 1st person to 2nd person. 
            [ SUB + V1 + OBJ ]
for 3rd person. 
            [ SUB + V1 (s/es/ies) + OBJ ]

Future Indefinet :-

Future indefinite tense is also known as future simple tense. This tense is used to express an action that will occur in the future. Such an action is intended to be done in the near or far future after being said about it by the speaker.
for positive sentance 
            [ SUB + WILL + V1 ]
for negative sentance 
            [ SUB + WILL NOT + V1 ]
